Industrial Pipeline Robot

The Vision

Autonomous navigation and cleaning system for high-risk industrial infrastructure.

The Problem

Traditional inspection methods are labor-intensive, costly, and pose risks to human workers. Sediment buildup and cracks lead to environmental disasters.

Objectives

• Motorized Scrubbing
• ESP32 Intelligence
• Wireless Monitoring

System Solution

Integrating ESP32 Controllers with L298N drivers for precise torque. Real-time feedback via Wi-Fi CAM.
